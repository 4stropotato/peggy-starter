# Higashi-Oda Elementary School — Registration Steps (Simple Guide)

This guide shows the correct order of procedures for school enrollment in Kawasaki City.

Follow in order to avoid missing requirements.

---

# STEP 1 — Login / Create Kawasaki Online Account

Portal:
https://lgpos.task-asp.net/cu/141305/ea/residents/portal/home

## Why first?
All later procedures use this system.

## What to do
1. Open the link.
2. Login or create account.
3. Confirm you can access procedures.

After login works, continue.

---

# STEP 2 — School Accident / Disaster Insurance

Information pages:
https://www.jpnsport.go.jp/anzen/
https://www.jpnsport.go.jp/anzen/saigai/seido/tabid/60/Default.aspx

Application form:
https://lgpos.task-asp.net/cu/141305/ea/residents/procedures/apply/1dae969f-45b5-4ae2-b64a-f5e3c7ab336c/start

## Explanation
Insurance covers:
- injury at school
- accidents during commute
- school activity injuries

Almost all students join.

## What to do
Submit guardian consent.

---

# STEP 3 — Student Information & Consent Forms

These forms register student data.

## 3.1 Child Personal Information
https://logoform.jp/f/du8fK

Enter:
- student details
- guardian contact
- emergency info

---

## 3.2 Personal Data Consent
https://logoform.jp/form/FUQz/495669

Permission for school to use student data.

---

## 3.3 Online Education / Media Consent
https://logoform.jp/form/FUQz/495558

Permission for:
- photos
- online class tools
- activity records

You may allow or refuse.

---

# STEP 4 — School Lunch Application

Application:
https://lgpos.task-asp.net/cu/141305/ea/residents/procedures/apply/dff59dc3-3b43-47d3-a0bb-a0b04214bd63/start

Information:
https://www.city.kawasaki.jp/880/page/0000121366.html

## Explanation
School lunch is standard.

If not applied:
child must bring lunch daily.

## What to do
Submit:
- participation
- allergy info
- diet restrictions

---

# STEP 5 — Bank Account for School Payments

Bank procedure guide:
https://www.kawashin.co.jp/etc/kakunin.html

## Explanation
School fees are automatically withdrawn.

Used for:
- lunch
- insurance
- materials

## What to do
Ensure guardian has Kawashin account.

If none:
open account at branch.

---

# STEP 6 — Registration Complete

After finishing:
✔ student registered  
✔ insurance active  
✔ lunch registered  
✔ payment ready  

Next steps:
- entrance ceremony
- prepare school items
- start school

---

# Recommended Pace (not overwhelming)

Day 1 — Portal + Insurance  
Day 2 — Student forms  
Day 3 — Lunch application  
Day 4 — Bank confirmation
